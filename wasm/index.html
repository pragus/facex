How it works

FaceX runs a neural network entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your camera feed never leaves your device — no server, no cloud, no tracking.

The engine converts each face into 512 numbers (an "embedding"). When two embeddings are similar enough, it's the same person.

Engine size: 46 KB WASM. Model weights loaded separately.
